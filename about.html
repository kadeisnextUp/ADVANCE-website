About ADVANCE

Who We Are

ADVANCE is a minority professional development organization founded by African American students at the University of Cincinnati in 1986, in response to the expressed need of minority students to enhance their career awareness and preparation for career success. Advance was inspired by the objectives of the Corporate Orientation Program (CORP).

Our Mission

ADVANCE's overall goal is to prepare college students for a world of effective professionalism and to develop a cohesive organization for the ADVANCEMENT of students at the University of Cincinnati. ADVANCE is an educational program under the auspices of the UC Career Development Center.

Goals

To provide students with the tools and resources they need to succeed in their careers, including mentorship, networking opportunities, and professional development workshops.

Benefits to Students

ADVANCE offers a range of benefits to its members, including access to exclusive events, workshops, and networking opportunities with industry professionals.

Programs & Events

We host workshops, info sessions, and networking events throughout the year to help students connect with employers and alumni.
