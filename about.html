About ADVANCE

Who We Are

ADVANCE is a minority professional development organization founded by African American students at the University of Cincinnati in 1986, in response to the expressed need of minority students to enhance their career awareness and preparation for career success. Advance was inspired by the objectives of the CORP.

Our Mission

ADVANCE's overall goal is to prepare college students for a world of effective professionalism and to develop a cohesive organization for the ADVANCEMENT of students at the University of Cincinnati. ADVANCE is an educational program under the auspices of the UC Career Development Center.

Goals

To provide students with the tools and resources they need to succeed in their careers, including mentorship, networking opportunities, and professional development workshops.

Benefits to Students

By joining ADVANCE, students gain access to a supportive community of like-minded individuals, as well as opportunities to connect with industry professionals and alumni. Members also receive exclusive access to job and internship postings, as well as discounts on events and workshops.

Programs & Events

ADVANCE hosts a variety of programs and events throughout the year, such as networking receptions, professional development workshops and a corporate excursion. The organization also partners with local businesses and organizations to provide members with real-world experience and opportunities to give back to the community.
