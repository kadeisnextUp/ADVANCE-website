About ADVANCE

ADVANCE is a minority professional development organization founded by African American students at the University of Cincinnati in 1986, in response to the expressed need of minority students to enhance their career awareness and preparation for career success. Advance was inspired by the objectives of the Corporate Orientation Program (CORP), which then operated under the Council on Career Development for Minorities, Inc...
